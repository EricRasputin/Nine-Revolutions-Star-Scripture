# Chapter 16: City Lord Li Beiye's Choice

At that moment, outside Yebei City.

A figure flickered through the void, drawing closer and closer to Yebei City.

"Yebei City! I, Su Yang, have returned."

*Wang Yiyi, when the time comes, I hope you won't be too surprised. The words I, Su Yang, have spoken will come true!* Atop the Purple Lightning Mad Lion, Su Yang's fists clenched, no small excitement in his chest.

Three months had passed, and at last he was back in Yebei City.

Only this time was unlike the last. Last time he had crawled in like a dead dog. This time he would come as an Asura.

He would settle it all at once: three years of hatred, three years of pain, every score with the Wang Family.

"Master, we're inside the city. Where do we go now?" The Purple Lightning Mad Lion landed on the roof of a commoner's house.

"No rush. Let me feel it out." Su Yang closed his eyes. Three years had nearly worn the Wang Manor's exact location from his memory.

Soon enough, Su Yang sensed several powerful surges of spiritual energy from the north and turned to look that way, only for his brow to furrow. "That direction... isn't that the Su Family?"

*What's going on?* Suspicion stirred in Su Yang's heart. He didn't know the current state of Yebei City, but now was as good a moment as any to find out.

With his current cultivation, so long as he masked his presence, he should escape notice.

With that, Su Yang patted the Purple Lightning Mad Lion. "Head north. Don't let anyone see us."

"Mm!" The Purple Lightning Mad Lion grunted assent and dissolved into a black streak that vanished into the dark.

At that moment, inside the Su Manor.

Seeing that Wang Lang had finally shown his true colors, killing intent pouring off him, Su Beilou had no choice but to act. "Wang Lang. If you mean to wipe out the Su Family, you'll have to step over Su Beilou's corpse first."

Boom!

A rank-three Divine Martial Realm aura burst out of him.

Even so, set against the aura Wang Lang had unleashed, it fell well short.

"Brother Beilou, I didn't expect you'd come this far in the years since we last crossed hands." Wang Lang's mouth curled in a mocking smile.

"Save your breath. Make your move." Knowing he had no retreat left, Su Beilou struck first. Spiritual energy washed over his body and formed a shield around him, Tri-Profound qi swirling out.

His aged frame surged with boundless power, and he drove a fist toward the spot where Wang Lang and the others stood.

It came on with the momentum of a charging tiger.

This was a mid-grade Su Family technique, the Fierce Tiger Fist!

With a roar, the powerful aura tore outward like a gale and slammed into Wang Lang's position in an instant.

The blast shook the entire Su Manor.

Soon enough, the rest of the Su Family's experts gathered at Su Beilou's side, his closest confidants.

The Su Family's Second Elder, Su Qiang.

The Su Family's Third Elder, Su Mo.

And the Su Family's Fourth Elder, Su Chen!

All three were at peak Tri-Profound Realm, and all three were glaring at Wang Lang. Su Qiang jabbed a finger in his face. "You son of a bitch, Wang Lang! Have you forgotten so quickly how you came begging us to help your Wang Family back in the day?"

Su Beilou's strike had done nothing to wound Wang Lang or the others, only blasting out a wide crater, and Wang Lang's whole group hung in the void, watching the Su Family men as if watching clowns.

"It seems everyone's accounted for."

"Very well, a family should go down to hell together, every last one of them."

"Strike!"

At this point Wang Lang no longer hesitated, and he sent in the rest of his peak Tri-Profound Realm Wang Family experts.

Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh! Three Wang Family experts, all of them at peak Tri-Profound Realm, surged in to attack the Su Family men who had just appeared.

"Hmph, Wang Zixiang, this old man has long wanted to take your dog's life." Seeing a Wang Family expert at the same realm closing on him, Su Qiang let out a cold snort.

"Death's on your doorstep, and you still talk this big?" Wang Zixiang of the Wang Family let out a contemptuous laugh.

Boom!

The two clashed fists, and both staggered backward in the same instant.

At the same time, Su Mo and Su Chen joined battle with the other two Wang Family experts.

The collision of powerful spiritual energies had already torn the Su Manor apart!

In that moment, the whole of Yebei City had fallen silent. The common folk dared not stir at all and could only pray under their breath that the conflict would not reach their lives.

At the southernmost edge of Yebei City, inside the City Lord's Manor.

Li Beiye stood on the roof in full battle armor, looking toward the Su Family in the north, and said softly, "So it's finally begun?"

Beside Li Beiye stood a slender figure, her expression taut with strain. "Father... is the Wang Family really going to wipe out the Su Family and our City Lord's Manor?" Her voice was soft, with a trace of fear in it.

"Shanshan, there's no time to lose, leave the city at once. Go to your aunt's, and only then will your father rest easy." Li Beiye stroked his daughter's lovely black hair, his voice gentle and reluctant to let her go.

Were there any other choice, who would willingly tear flesh and blood apart?

"No! Father, this daughter won't go. This daughter is staying by Father's side!" The young woman's tone was firm, but a sob had already crept into her voice.

This was Li Beiye's beloved daughter, the bride-to-be Su Yang had been blind enough to refuse all those years ago: Li Shanshan!

Beneath a long gown white as snow was a face like a porcelain doll's, except just now her delicate brows were lightly drawn together, bringing a faint trace of worry to her fine features, and that touch of pity made her already striking beauty all the more affecting.

If Su Yang could see Li Shanshan in this moment, he would surely curse himself in silence, not only for being blind back then but for having let pig fat smear his heart.[^1]

"Shanshan, listen to me. All these years your father has hardly let you set foot outside this manor, first because I feared danger reaching you, second to keep you safe."

"Now that the Wang Manor has risen, that wretch Wang Lang will not let us go. This City Lord's Manor I built with my own two hands, and no matter what comes your father will not leave it lightly, but you, you are the pearl in my palm."

"And there is one more thing it's time you knew."

At that, complicated feelings stole into Li Beiye's expression, as if he were still weighing whether to tell Li Shanshan the truth.

"Father... you..." For a moment Li Shanshan was at a loss, her hands tightening around the hem of her gown.

"Ah, by now you must have noticed yourself, all these years your cultivation has not broken through, and that is because of the Extreme Yin Bloodline within you. Your father has tried every method these years and found no answer to the bloodline in your body, and once upon a time I had hoped that the Su Family's prodigy, Su Yang with his Supreme Bloodline, would marry you so that his bloodline could counterbalance the Extreme Yin in your body, but unfortunately... fate twisted, and that boy chose Wang Yiyi."

"He must surely regret it now."

At those words, a cool breeze passed through father and daughter's black hair, lifting what seemed to be the same memory in both of them...

Li Shanshan pressed her lips together, as if a figure she hadn't seen in three years had surfaced in her mind.

"Su... Yang," Li Shanshan whispered.

"Enough, there's no more time, I'll have General Yang see you out at once." Li Beiye, not wanting to lose another moment, clapped his hands, and a soldier in silver battle armor appeared on the roof.

"My Lord!" The soldier knelt in salute.

"General Yang, there's no time to lose, take Shanshan now and head for the Liang Manor in Middle Province, where someone will be waiting for you."

With that, Li Beiye drew out a dark-green command token and pressed it into General Yang's hand.

General Yang took it in both hands and raised his head, his voice unwilling. "My Lord, let us leave together, and I'll lead the brothers in cutting a bloody path through them! This general will see you and Lady Shanshan out of Yebei City, even if it costs me my life."

Seeing such loyalty and devotion in the general who had stood by him for so many years, Li Beiye broke into a relieved smile. "To have known a brother like you in this life, I, Li Beiye, can die without regret!"

With that, Li Beiye took up a long spear, the cape on his battle armor billowing in the wind, his whole body like a god of war, and leapt away toward the Su Family in the north.

---

[^1]: 猪油蒙了心 (zhū yóu méng le xīn) — literally "pig fat smeared his heart." A vivid Chinese idiom for being so muddled in judgment that you can't see what's right in front of you, as if grease had clouded your inner senses. The image is rural and unflattering: not just blind, but greasily, congealedly blind. Su Yang's imagined self-rebuke is not "I was blind"; it's "I was *that kind* of blind."
