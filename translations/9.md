# Chapter 9: Purple Lightning Laser Cannon

At those words, Su Yang sucked in a cold breath despite himself.

*If I really could subdue this Purple Lightning Mad Lion, my grand revenge plan would become a great deal easier.*

*This thing taking on Wang Lang? No problem at all.*

As for Wang Yiyi, Su Yang was set on dealing with her himself and reclaiming the Supreme Bloodline that rightfully belonged to him.

"What do I need to do?" Su Yang asked.

"What's so hard about that? Smash him and we're done. First, let this demonic beast feel your overwhelming combat strength; let it submit to your presence. Then this Great Sage will have his own way of bringing the Purple Lightning Mad Lion to heel."

"..."

Before Su Yang could even respond, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion had already launched its attack. Its crouched body sprang upward, and its massive, vicious claws hooked toward Su Yang as if to rip a hole in the empty air.

Su Yang's spiritual energy erupted; he braced the staff in his hands across his chest.

A thunderous boom, and a tremendous force surged through the wooden staff and into both of Su Yang's arms.

A numbness shot through every limb in an instant.

The sheer impact sent Su Yang's body flying back several meters, but he steadied his stance just in time.

"Damn it!" Su Yang spat, spiritual energy bursting from him; gripping his qi-wreathed staff, he struck back.

His massive staff swept skyward, then crashed down.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion was every bit as quick. Su Yang's blow met empty air; the lion roared again, vicious claws lunging for Su Yang's head.

Su Yang flung the staff into the air, the Battle Sage Art igniting within him. His fists clenched tight, and the strength of ten thousand jin[^1] flooded every part of him.

"Hyah!!" Facing the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's vicious claws, Su Yang threw a savage punch.

A pure clash of bodies.

Bam!

Fist and claw slammed together, wave after wave of fierce wind erupting outward.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion's pupils shifted in spite of itself. It hadn't imagined the seemingly frail human in front of it would dare clash body-to-body. And yet, here he was, holding his ground without losing an inch.

Its kingly pride curdled into rage.

"Roaaaaar!"

Another savage roar. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion burst with another wave of terrifying demonic aura, and Su Yang's expression couldn't help but twist.

"Hyah!" Su Yang too poured every ounce of strength into the strike, pushing his physical force to its absolute limit, an overwhelming combat aura erupting from him. In an instant, he sent the Purple Lightning Mad Lion flying!!!

It flapped its wings frantically to right itself, landing steady on the ground. This time, though, it did not launch another attack. It crouched again, body low, eyes locked dead on Su Yang.

Su Yang had spent most of his strength on that blow, yet hadn't done any real damage to the Purple Lightning Mad Lion. The fierce light in his eyes, though, hadn't dimmed in the slightest.

He kept his fists clenched, body still primed for the next attack.

"Roaaar—"

Sensing it had been scorned by this human in front of it, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion let out a roar, and its already-purple fur crackled with the sound of lightning.

*I'm a proper rank-six demonic beast, and a feeble human is going to scorn me?*

*I have to tear him apart!!*

Knowing full well the rank-six beast was about to unleash a beast technique, Su Yang didn't dare grow careless. He scrambled to retrieve the staff, spiritual energy raging out from him.

"Feeble human, you must die!!"

Astonishingly, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion had spoken in a human tongue. Killing intent blazed in its furious eyes.

Lightning had wreathed every strand of fur on its body.

"Hmph, we'll see who dies! Submit to me obediently and I might consider sparing your life." Su Yang knew that some demonic beasts who reached rank-six could indeed speak in human tongue, and once they reached rank-seven and above, they could shapeshift into human form.

"Die!!!"

"Purple Lightning Laser Cannon[^2]!"

As the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's words fell, a terrifying wave of purple lightning energy burst out from its body.

That kind of force was enough to obliterate any expert below the Divine Martial Realm. Even a Divine Martial Realm expert hit by it would suffer critical injuries, if not be killed outright.

Su Yang stared at the incoming attack, his expression grim, the combat imagery of the Battle Sage Art surfacing in his mind.

The staff in his hand whirled and spun, weaving into a shield of staff afterimages.

The Purple Lightning Laser Cannon's force slammed into the staff barrier in an instant. The sheer impact sent Su Yang's body skidding backward at blistering speed.

Everything behind him that might have offered cover dissolved into nothing under the lion's attack.

Su Yang's expression was grim. His swinging arms had long since started to tremble, and even the qi-wreathed staff in his grip was beginning to crack apart, proof of just how staggering this power truly was.

"Tsk-tsk, this Purple Lightning Mad Lion is quite vicious to muster a force this overwhelming, and mind you, it isn't even fully grown yet. Kid, don't get careless. Don't forget the real meaning of the Battle Sage Art; if you trade qi blasts with this thing, you don't have enough lives to die."

The Great Sage's words rose in Su Yang's mind, as if reminding him of something.

Hearing this, Su Yang understood at once what Senior Great Sage meant.

The Battle Sage Art was a method of fighting at its core, not of qi-clashing. It could only be advanced through ceaseless physical combat, by stoking the strength inside him until he pushed past every limit and shattered the chains within his body, so each fight made him braver and each act of courage made him stronger.

"Hyaaaah!!!"

Even as the force flung Su Yang nearly a thousand meters back, he let out a roar of fury that tore through the heavens like a thunderclap.

Every demonic beast of rank-four or below in the vicinity scattered in panic.

Every drop of spiritual energy in his body erupted at once, golden radiance and purple lightning fusing into one and blooming like dazzling fireworks...

Boom—

The roar of the blast was deafening.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion took in the scene and curled its mouth into a sneer. "A feeble human dares challenge this King's authority? After all the time I've let you live, dying under this King's Purple Lightning Laser Cannon should be cause enough for pride."

To the lion's mind, the human youth from a moment ago had to have been ground to dust under its attack.

Just as it gave its purple fur a shake and readied itself to leave...

A voice rose from afar, drawing nearer. "Hey, little lion. Don't be in such a rush, yours truly isn't dead yet!!!"

As the words fell, the lion froze mid-shake. Disbelief flooded its pupils as it stared at the kilometer-long stretch of devastation directly ahead. There, walking toward it bare-handed, was a youth wrapped in golden radiance, his clothes in tatters.

"This... how is this possible!!!"

"This human is still alive? And he's only at the True Martial Realm, so how could he stand against this Mad Lion's Purple Lightning Laser Cannon?"

"Damn it, what is going on? Has this Mad Lion's power weakened?"

The lion, so arrogant a moment before, watched Su Yang walking toward it alive, and a wave of frustration surged into its heart.

*A proper rank-six demonic beast, and I can't even one-shot a single True Martial Realm human cultivator? My title of king is nothing but empty words.*

Before it could fully grasp the reality of Su Yang being alive, the youth was already standing in front of it again.

Eyes feral as a beast's, Su Yang fixed his gaze on the Purple Lightning Mad Lion. "Little lion. Up for another round?"

---

[^1]: 万斤 (wàn jīn): "ten thousand jin." A 斤 (jīn) is a classical Chinese unit of weight, roughly 600 grams. Ten thousand jin works out to about six metric tons — a stock xianxia hyperbole for monstrous physical strength. Cultivation novels measure raw force in jin the way Western fantasy measures it in "the strength of a hundred men."
[^2]: 镭射 (léi shè) — the Chinese transliteration of the English word "laser," made by phonetically borrowing two characters that literally mean "radium-shoot." Using a modern loanword inside a traditional martial-cultivation technique name is a deliberate stylistic choice by the author: it gives the rank-six beast's signature attack a distinctly sci-fi register against the surrounding xianxia idiom.
