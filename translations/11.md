# Chapter 11: The Mad Lion's Fear

"Human brat, do you not know when to quit? Don't think this King is actually scared of you!" Watching Su Yang still on its tail, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion was beyond frustrated. It couldn't help but regret ever picking a fight with this reckless human boy.

Su Yang didn't bother answering the lion at all. If anything, his pace picked up another notch.

This was the wonder of the Battle Sage Art.

Not only did it raise the practitioner's combat strength at the fastest possible pace, but his actual speed could multiply several times over in short order.

Otherwise, with Su Yang's actual cultivation realm, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion would have left him miles behind.

The chase had stretched nearly fifty kilometers by now. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion was almost at the deep mountains. Once there, it could lead the human kid to one of its old rivals, and the rival would finish him off without the lion needing to lift a paw.

Su Yang knew, though, that the longer this dragged on, the worse it got for him.

Who knew what other terrifying beasts lurked in the depths of the Demonic Beast Mountain Range.

He clenched his teeth and forced the Supreme Bloodline's power to flow through him. Heedless of the massive backlash now ravaging his body, his speed doubled again, and in the blink of an eye he was right on the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's tail.

Sensing the presence closing in behind it, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion despaired aloud. "Damn it all, where the hell did this freak of a kid come from?"

*Whoosh—*

The words had barely left its mouth when twin flashes of light burst, and Su Yang and the Purple Lightning Mad Lion vanished into the void.

Only a small black stone was left, plummeting through the air... but then, who would have cared to notice?

Two figures plunged straight into the starry world inside the stone. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion happened to land right beside the Great Sage's statue, and as it staggered to its feet, it cursed. "Damn it! Where the hell am I? What did that damned kid do to me?"

When the Purple Lightning Mad Lion finally took in the scene around it, it jolted upright. Its eyes went wide as bronze bells, its jaw fell open, and it stood frozen on the spot, lost as to what to do.

"That kid really is fierce. Even this Great Sage is impressed. He forced the Supreme Bloodline's power without caring about the backlash on himself, and that alone is enough to make this Sage see him in a whole new light." The Great Sage's voice rang out, filling the starry sky.

Already dazed, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion now heard a voice out of nowhere. It dropped into a crouch and swept its gaze around, on full alert. "Who? Who's speaking? Show yourself before this King at once!"

"Little kitty, this Sage is right next to you. Eh? You see this Sage and you still haven't dropped to your knees?"

On those words, a tremendous pressure surged out of the statue. At the same time, a tremor born of bloodline suppression rippled through the lion, and its knees hit the ground in an instant as it trembled like a leaf.

In its heart, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion knew a fear it couldn't even put into words.

*What is happening today? I finally caught a human kid, and it turned out he wasn't ordinary at all, and he's a goddamn lunatic on top of it. Now somehow I've ended up in this strange place, and... this fear from a higher bloodline, what is even going on...*

"Little kitty, you have just two choices in front of you. One: you let this Sage destroy your body and soul on the spot. Two: you submit to that human kid lying unconscious next to you."

"Of course, this Sage knows you're a mutated rank-six demonic beast. But as you can see, the human kid next to you is no ordinary one, and he might just turn out to be your lucky star down the road. This Great Sage won't force the choice on you. Three breaths is all you get. Live or die, the call is yours."

Hearing this, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's already-trembling body shook even harder.

It didn't doubt a word the speaker had said. Anyone with bloodline pressure this strong had to be at least a rank-eight demonic beast Senior. Maybe even the King who slumbered somewhere deep in the Demonic Beast Mountain Range itself...

In just three breaths, the lion's mind hadn't even started working.

"Hmm? Looks like you don't think much of your own life." The Great Sage's voice rose again.

That soul-shaking pressure made the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's fur stand on end all over again.

Its once-proud head was now pressed flat against the ground, its voice trembling. "I... I submit!"

"Heh-heh, good thing this kitty knows what's good for it. I'd say one day you'll be thanking yourself for the choice you made today."

With that, the pressure faded.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion, prostrate on the ground, sprang back to its feet at once. But it kept its head bowed, not daring to look the Great Sage's statue in the face.

"If this junior may ask, Senior... what does this junior need to do?" The Purple Lightning Mad Lion had never imagined a day when it would submit to a human cultivator. With no idea whether anything else was expected of it, it could only ask in a meek voice.

"For a start, offer up a bit of your essence blood. The kid is in a terrible state right now and won't wake for half a month otherwise. If he can absorb your essence blood, he'll recover much faster. Once he wakes, this Sage will have the two of you sign a Soul Contract. From that point on, you'll be by his side forever, and the only way you'll ever leave him is if the kid himself releases you and dissolves the contract on his own."

"Understood?"

"This junior understands. This junior understands."

Naturally, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion didn't dare doubt a word from this mysterious Senior. As for a bit of essence blood, what was that worth? Its very life was about to belong to the human boy anyway...

So the Purple Lightning Mad Lion didn't drag its feet. It walked over to the unconscious Su Yang at once, lifted its right paw, and drove a claw into its own palm. A few drops of essence blood welled out in an instant and fell into Su Yang's mouth.

As the essence blood went down Su Yang's throat, his weakened aura immediately began to mend, bit by bit.

The wounds on his body began to close at a pace the eye could actually follow.

Such was the power of a demonic beast's essence blood. Anything from a rank-nine beast or higher, though, would have likely blown Su Yang apart on the spot.

Seeing the Purple Lightning Mad Lion this obedient, the Great Sage didn't bother saying any more, as if simply waiting for Su Yang to come around...

Watching the human youth in front of it, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion couldn't shake the suspicion that this kid was some demonic beast Senior's reincarnation. But it wasn't feeling any bloodline suppression off him, which meant he really was just an ordinary human boy, only with blood that was a little different from other humans'.

Could that be what made him special?

Of course, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion had no idea what a Supreme Bloodline was. Even less could it understand why a demonic beast Senior this powerful was looking out so closely for the human boy on the ground.

"Cough, cough!"

Two coughs rang out, and bloody foam sprayed from his lips. Su Yang slowly opened his eyes, shut them, opened them again, blinking back and forth until his vision was finally clear. Only then did he straighten up.

When he saw the enormous lion crouched right beside him, he almost jumped out of his skin. Luckily, Su Yang caught himself quickly, remembering what he had done earlier, and let out a breath of relief. "Phew. For a second there I thought I was about to kick the bucket again..."
